Our History

Vallejo Public Library started a long time ago when people in the community wanted a place for books and learning. They opened the library in 19xx with just a few donated books. Even when tough things happened, like fires, the library kept going. Now, it's a big part of Vallejo, with lots of books and fun things for everyone. It's a place where people can learn and grow together.
